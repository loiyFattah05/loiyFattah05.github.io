Website Evaluations

Evalutation of two different websites using the CRAP principles

Apple — Good Example

Apple’s website Follows the CRAP principles very carefully. Very clean website and very easy to navigate/reach the desired destination/product.

  • Contrast: Excellent — Big images against black or white backgrounds. This combination makes everything pop.
  • Repetition: Consistent layout, fonts, and buttons. Everything mstches perfectly.
  • Alignment: Everything is lined up, text, images, and buttons all float clean sections.
  • Proximity: All related things are grouped together with lots of space to be able to clearly distinguish between products/services..

Paradise Drinking Water — Bad Example

This site is horrible website regarding the CRAP principles. It still works, but horrible.

  • Contrast: Alot of blue on white blocks. Alot of different blocks all over the screen making really nothing stand out.
  • Repetition: Different fonts in each part of the screen. Different heading all over the screen going for h1 to h3 back to h2 and so on.
  • Alignment: The page looks like a stcaked flyer, images, lists,and subheads all sit on different axes..
  • Proximity: None of the related stuff are grouped together. The page is a bunch of random blocks. Ex. An introduction has a promo right next to it which doesn't make sense
