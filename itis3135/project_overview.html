Client Project - Part 1: Requirements & Design Proposal

Project Overview

Application Name: Palestinian Cultural Organization

Purpose: represent the Palestinian Cultural Organization at UNC Charlotte. Promotes awareness of Palestinian culture, share updates about events and activities, and provide a space for members and visitors to learn, connect, and participate in the club’s community.

Intended Users: Mainly UNC Students who are interested in learning about the organization, but also more broadly for faculty and community members who don't get to hear about the organization around campus

Overview of Content: The website will include a homepage displaying the organization’s emblem and a brief introduction. Other pages will provide details about the organization, its members, upcoming events, and sponsorship opportunities, along with ways to contact or get involved.
